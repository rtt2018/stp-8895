About This Game

Plane Merge: Idle Merging Game is a relaxing and rewarding idle merger where you manage your very own aircraft empire

Start with a single plane, and through intelligent merging and upgrading, build a sky-dominating fleet. Each aircraft brings something new to the table, and every upgrade gets you closer to aviation greatness. Whether you play for a few minutes or for hours, the fun is non-stop!
